Bike Rental Services

Fietsverhuur Den Haag (The Hague Bike Rental)

Website:Fietsverhuur Den Haag

Location: Multiple locations in The Hague

view on map

Fietsverhuur Den Haag, also known as The Hague Bike Rental, is your go-to destination for renting a bicycle in the city. They offer a wide range of options, including standard city bikes for daily commuting, electric bikes (e-bikes) for effortless exploration, and cargo bikes for those who need extra storage or are cycling with family. Whether you need a bike for a day or an extended period, they have flexible rental options to suit your needs.

OV-fiets

Website:Ov-Fiets

Location:Various train stations in The Hague

view on map

If you're using public transportation in The Hague, consider the OV-fiets service. It's part of the Dutch public transportation system (NS) and allows you to rent bikes at train stations, making it convenient for commuters and travelers. To use this service, you'll need an OV-chipkaart, which is the Netherlands' public transportation card.
