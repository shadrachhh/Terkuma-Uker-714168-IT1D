Bike Maintenance

Basic Maintenance

Regular Check-ups

Just like cars need regular check-ups, bicycles benefit from routine maintenance. This includes checking tire pressure, brakes, gears, and lubricating moving parts. You can perform these tasks yourself or have a local bike shop do it for a small fee, typically ranging from €10 to €30 for a basic service.

Cleaning Supplies

Keeping your bike clean is essential for longevity. Expect to spend a few euros on cleaning brushes, chain degreasers, and lubricants.

Repairs

Flat Tires

Flat tires are a common issue. You can either fix them yourself with a patch kit (a few euros) or have a bike shop repair or replace the tube (typically around €10 to €20 per tire).

Brake Pads

Brake pads wear out over time. Replacement brake pads cost roughly €5 to €15 per pair, and installation is usually straightforward.

Chain and Gears

If your chain or gears become worn, replacements can range from €10 to €30 for the chain and €15 to €40 for the cassette or freewheel. Labor costs for installation vary by bike shop.
